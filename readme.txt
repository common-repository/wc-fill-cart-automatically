Fill your customers cart with the products you choose, it can be filled automatically or through a link.

== Description ==

Easily fill your customers cart.

You can customize the products with which you want to fill the cart.

You can choose if you want it to be auto-filled or to be filled using a link.

Provides a section to view information on the number of users who have filled their cart and information about the user.
